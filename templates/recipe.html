My Ginger Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 350g golden syrup
  • 125g unsalted butter
  • 250g sieved plain flour
  • 1 and 3 quarter teaspoons bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 and 3 quarter teaspoons mixed spice
  • 1 large egg
  • 275 ml milk

How to make it:

  1. Set the oven to 180 degrees C, 350 degrees F, fan 170 degrees C, gas mark 4.
  2. Grease a tin (18x28x4cm) with butter and line with greaseproof paper, then grease the paper too.
  3. Put the golden syrup and butter in a pan and place over a low heat until the butter has melted.
  4. Measure the flour, bicarbonate of soda, ginger and mixed spice into a bowl.
  5. Gradually add the syrup and butter, mixing thoroughly.
  6. In a separate bowl, beat the egg annd milk together.
  7. Add the egg and milk to the main mixture bit by bit, mixing well. The mixture should be very runny.
  8. Pour the mix into the tin and bake in the centre of the oven for 50 minutes.
  9. Cool in the tin for 10-15 minutes then turn out onto a wire tray.
  10. Cut the cake into squares, decorating them if you want, and store in an airtight container ready to eat!
